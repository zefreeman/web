I am an incoming postdoc at the Psychology of Digitalisation department, University of Bern. My research is in mental health and meta-research. My PhD was based in a randomised controlled trial at King's College London, where I investigated trauma memory in people with both psychosis and PTSD.

I value working in and contributing to a positive, inclusive research culture. For people at an early career stage, particularly from groups under-represented in academia, it can be hard to reach out. If this is you, please do contact me to discuss research or careers.
