Teaching


I created, co-convene, and co-teach the Open and Responsible Research course to PhD and postdoctoral researchers on the London Interdisciplinary Social Sciences Doctoral Training Partnership programme.

I teach on the MSc Mental Health Studies on the topics of definitions of trauma, generative AI in research, and (how to not do) plagiarism.

I also supervise MSc research project students and guest lecture on research credibility. With this work, I am particularly interested in methodological transparency and considered approaches to measuring experiences of marginalisation and inequality.
