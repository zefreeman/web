Teaching


PhD and postdoctoral: I created, co-convene, and co-teach the Open and Responsible Research course to researchers on the London Interdisciplinary Social Sciences Doctoral Training Partnership programme.

MSc: I teach on the MSc Mental Health Studies at King's College London on how psychological trauma is defined and measured, the use of generative AI in research, and (how to not do) plagiarism. I also supervise MSc research student dissertation projects.

I guest lecture on research credibility, methodological transparency, and considered approaches to measuring marginalisation.
