Teaching


PhD and postdoctoral: I created, co-convene, and co-teach the Open and Responsible Research course to researchers on the London Interdisciplinary Social Sciences Doctoral Training Partnership programme. We recently converted this course into an online format.

MSc: I taught on the MSc Mental Health Studies at King's College London on how psychological trauma is defined and measured, the use of generative AI in research, and (how to not do) plagiarism. I also supervised MSc Neuroscience student dissertation projects.

I give talks and guest lectures on measurement, mental health, and the credibility of research.
